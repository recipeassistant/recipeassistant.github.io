Rishda

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (1 cup)
  • Lamb (600g)
  • Tomato Puree (4 tbsp/74g)
  • Onions (5)
  • Water for sauce (1 litre)
  • SPICES:
  • Cinnamon (1 tbsp/4g)
  • Chilli powder (1 tbsp/4g)
  • Tumeric (1 tbsp/4g)
  • Ginger (1 tbsp/4g)
  • Clove (1 tbsp/4g)

Directions

Step 1:

Soak the chickpeas over night. Cover the pasta in boiling water in a deep pan, simmer till done 7-10 mins. Add oil in a big pan and add 1 onion cut into small pieces. Cook for abit then add 3 tablespoons of tomatoe paste and cook for a few mins


Step 2:

Then add the spices and mix. Then add the meat and cook on low heat for a few mins whilst stirring. Then add around 1 litre of boiling water and simmer till the meat is cooked.


Step 3:

Add abit of oil to a saucepan, and cook the rest of the 4 onions cut into thin slices for a few mins. Then add 1 tbsp of tumeric, and the chickpeas and simmer for a few mins. Then add a spoon of butter, a 4-6 ladles of the meat sauce, tbsp of cinnamon and simmer for a few mins.


Step 4:

Once the angel pasta is cooked, add abit of the oil from the meat sauce bit by bit and run it through the pasta in sections and mix well. Then pour the meat stew over it, then the chickpeas sauce and serve!
