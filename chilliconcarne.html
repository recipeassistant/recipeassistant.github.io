Chilli Con Carne

Ingredients

  • Rice (400g)
  • Beef mince (500g)
  • Onion (1.5)
  • Pepper (1)
  • Coriander (garnish)
  • Chopped tomatos (400g)
  • Tomato Puree (3 tblsp)
  • Red Kidney beans (400g)
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Oregano
  • Garlic (2 cloves)

Directions

Step 1:

Heat oil in a pan and fry the mince meat till brown and just about cooked, season with salt and pepper as its cooking, then set aside in a bowl


Step 2:

Add the rice onto some oil in a deep sauecpan with (3 cups) boiling water, 2 tblsp salt and simmer till cooked 10-15 mins.


Step 2:

Meanwhile, Chop and dice the onions, pepper and chilli and grate the garlic. and fry all veg in the same pan in some oil till soft 4-5 mins


Step 2:

Add the tomato puree, 1 tsp of cumin, 1 tsp of dried oregano and 1.5 tsp of smoked paprika. Mix and fry for 2-3 mins.


Step 2:

Add the beef mince back in and the chopped tomatoes in the mix and simmer for 45-50 mins till thick and glossy


Step 2:

Add the kidney beans and season with salt and pepper and cook for another 4-5 mins, garnish with coriander, pour onto the rice & serve!
