Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (500g)
  • Yoghurt (240ml)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper (or more to taste)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • Heavy cream (250ml)
  • 1 tsp kasoori methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Directions

Step 1:

In a large bowl, mix together yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, cayenne

Step 2:

In a large bowl, mix together yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, cayenne pepper, and salt. Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight in the refrigerator.

Step 3:

Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger and saut√© until the onions are golden brown.

Step 4:

Add the diced tomatoes, heavy cream, kasoori methi, and sugar. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.

Step 5:

Add the marinated chicken to the pan and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

Step 6:

Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve over rice or with naan bread.
