Haloumi Burger

Ingredients

  • Pepper (2)
  • Haloumi cheese (2 blocks)
  • Burger buns (8)
  • Sweet chilli sauce

Directions

Step 1:

Deseed the pepper then slice in half and in half again to make 4 equal slices of pepper. do with both peppers for a total of 8 slices


Step 2:

Drizzle some oil onto the pepper slices and season with salt and pepper. Then pop into the air fryer at 200deg for 10 mins


Step 3:

Meanwhile, cut the haloumi cheese into 8 slices, heat oil in a pan and fry the haloumi cheese slices till golden, 4-8 mins, flipping every few mins


Step 4:

Add the burger buns into the air fryer for a few mins till toasted.


Step 5:

Then for each burger bun, add a slice of pepper, a slice of haloumi, some sweet chilli sauce and ketchup, and serve!.
