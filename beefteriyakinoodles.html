Beef Teriyaki Noodles

Ingredients

  • Beef (400g)
  • Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Green beans (150g)
  • Egg noodles (5 nests/250g)
  • Coriander (handful)
  • Chinese leaf (240g)
  • Ginger puree (60g)
  • Teriyaki sauce (300g)

Directions

Step 1:

Preheat oven to 240°C/220°C. Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil for the noodles, with 1/4 tsp salt. Trim the aubergine into 1cm chunks. Pop onto a large baking tray. Drizzle with oil, season with salt and pepper. Spread out & roast until soft and golden, 15-20 mins. Turn halway through.


Step 2:

Meanwhile, begin precooking your meat, trim the green beans and chop into thirds. Peel and grate the garlic. Roughly chop the coriander. Halve the chilli lengthways & chop. Set your coriander, chilli and peanuts aside for garnishing later.


Step 3:

When your pan of water is boiling, add the noodles and green beans and bring back to the boil. Cook until tender, 4 mins. Once cooked, drain in a colander. TIP: Run the noodles under cold water to stop them sticking together and to keep the beans vibrant.


Step 4:

Heat a drizzle of oil in a large frying pan on medium-high heat. Add the Chinese leaf and stirfry until softened, 3-4 mins. Add the meat, stir in the ginger puree and garlic, then stir-fry for 2 mins more.


Step 5:

Once the meat is cooked, pour in the teriyaki sauce and stir to coat, off the heat if necessary. Add the cooked noodles and green beans to the pan, then stir-fry until everything is well coated and piping hot, 1-2 mins. Taste and season if needed. Add a splash of water if it's a little thick. Top with as much coriander, chilli and peanuts as you'd like.
