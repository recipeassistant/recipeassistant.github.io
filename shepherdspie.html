Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup (240ml) beef or chicken broth
  • 1 cup (240ml) frozen peas
  • 1 cup (240ml) frozen corn
  • 1 teaspoon (5ml) thyme
  • 1 teaspoon (5ml) rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon (5ml) Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 cups (960ml) mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup (240ml) grated cheddar cheese

Directions:

Step 1:

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). In a large pan, cook the ground lamb or beef over medium heat until browned. Add diced onion and minced garlic to the pan and cook until the onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.

Step 2:

Add broth, frozen peas, frozen corn, thyme, rosemary, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper to the pan. Stir to combine and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 3:

While the meat mixture is simmering, prepare the mashed potatoes. Peel and dice 4 large potatoes. Boil the diced potatoes in a pot of salted water until they are tender, about 20 minutes. Drain the potatoes and mash with a potato masher or fork. Stir in a little milk and butter until the desired consistency is reached. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4:

Grease a 9x13 inch (23x33 cm) baking dish. Pour the meat mixture into the dish and spread it out evenly. Spoon the mashed potatoes on top of the meat mixture, spreading it out evenly. Sprinkle grated cheddar cheese on top of the mashed potatoes.

Step 5:

Bake the shepherd's pie in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the mashed potatoes are lightly browned. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.
