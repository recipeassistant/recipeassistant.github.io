Lamb Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice (400g)
  • Lamb (500g)
  • BlackPepper
  • Tumeric

Directions

Step 1:

Simmer bones in boiling water to use later to cook the rice with, for atleast a few hours


Step 2:

Use the stock water to cook the rice, leave in low heat with tbsp of salt for approx 15 mins.


Step 3:

once the rice is nearly done add more salt and tumeric


Step 4:

Cook the lamb in the air fryer at 190deg for 15 mins


Step 5:

Place the lamb onto the cooked rice and serve
