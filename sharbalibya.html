Sharba libya

Ingredients

  • Onion (1)
  • Lamb (500g)
  • Tomatoes (3)
  • Tomato puree (2 tbsp/35g)
  • Orzo pasta/Spaghetti (100g)
  • SPICES:
  • Clove (1.5 tsp/4g)
  • Nutmeg (0.5 tsp/1.5g)
  • Cinnamon (0.5 tsp/1.5g)
  • Cumin (0.5 tsp/1.5g)
  • Ginger (1 tsp/3g)
  • Paprika (0.5 tsp/1.5g)
  • Tumeric (1 tsp/3g)
  • Parsley (handful)
  • Mint (handful)

Directions

Step 1:

Cut and chop the lamb, tomatoes, parsley and onion into small pieces


Step 2:

In a pan, add 60ml of oil or butter, fry the lamb in oil with 1 big tsp each of clove, ginger, tumeric and salt, for a few minutes, then add the onion and continue frying for 3-4 minutes


Step 2:

Meanwhile, prepare your spice mix, in a bowl, mix half a teaspoon each of black pepper, cinnamon, clove, cumin, nutmeg and paprika.


Step 2:

Then in your pan, Stir in the parsley, tomatoes, tomato pur√©e, salt and the spice mix. Fry for about 4 minutes, stirring constantly, then add just enough water to cover the contents of the pan. between 400-700ml


Step 2:

Reduce the heat and simmer for about 20-30 mins, or until the meat is nearly cooked.


Step 2:

Add more water, if you like, then stir in the orzo pasta and cook on low heat until the pasta is soft 8-12 mins. add some lemon juice and the mint, then enjoy.
