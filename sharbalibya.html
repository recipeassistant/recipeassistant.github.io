Sharba libya

Ingredients

  • Onion (1/2)
  • Lamb (500g)
  • Tomato sauce (200g)
  • Tomato puree (1 tblsp/20g)
  • Clove (1 tblsp/6g)
  • Coriander (handful)
  • Tumeric (1 tblsp/6g)
  • Orzo pasta/Spaghetti (100g)
  • Mint (handful)
  • Water for sauce (750ml)

Directions

Step 1:

Cut and chop the lamb, coriander and onion into small pieces


Step 2:

In a pan, fry the lamb in oil with clove ginger and salt for a few minutes, then add the onion and continue frying for 5 minutes


Step 2:

Then add the tomato puree, tomato sauce and pasta and fry for a few minutes


Step 2:

Then add the coriander and water, close the lid and simmer for 15 mins


Step 2:

Add abit more clove, ginger and salt, turn off the heat garnish with mint and serve!
