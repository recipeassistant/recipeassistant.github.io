Kimchi Noodles

Ingredients

  • Onions (2)
  • Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Tomato sauce (4 tbsp)
  • Harissa (1 tbsp)
  • Sardines (2 tins)
  • Kimchi (1 Pot)
  • Angel hair pasta (500g)
  • Boiling water (1L)

Directions

Step 1:

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic cloves and fry until softened.


Step 2:

Properly wash the sardines with cold water in a sieve.


Step 3:

Add tomato sauce, harissa, kimchi and sardines to the pan and mix well.


Step 4:

Add 1 litre of boiling water, with salt, to the pan and stir. Cook for 5 minutes.


Step 5:

Add the desired amount of angel hair pasta to the pan and stir. Cook until pasta is fully cooked.
