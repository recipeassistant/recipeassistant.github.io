Pepper & Corn Taco

Ingredients

  • Pepper (2)
  • Grated cheese (120g)
  • Lime (1)
  • Greek cheese (100g)
  • Soured cream
  • Central american style spices
  • Taco tortilla (8)
  • Sweetcorn (200g)

Directions

Step 1:

Preheat your oven to 240°C/220°C. Drain the sweetcorn in a sieve. Chop the pepper into 1cm pieces. Heat oil in a pan and fry the pepper and corn until softened and starting to char, 5-6 mins.


Step 2:

Meanwhile, grate the Cheddar cheese. Crumble the Greek style salad cheese. Zest and halve the lime and add to a bowl.


Step 3:

Once the veg is cooked, remove from the heat and stir through the Central American style spice mix and add the cheeses and lime zest, Mix together well.


Step 4:

Lay the tortillas onto a baking tray and spoon the cheesy veg filling onto one half of each one. Fold the other side over, rub a little oil and bake till golden and crunchy 5-7 mins. and serve with sour cream or yoghurt as a dip.
