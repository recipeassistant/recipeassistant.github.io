Chicken burger & Cheese bites

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken (1lb)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Pepper (1 tsp)
  • Garlic powder (1 tsp)
  • Onion powder (1 tsp)
  • Breadcrumbs (1/2 cup)
  • Egg (1)
  • Buns (4)
  • Jalape√±o peppers (6-8, depending on size)
  • Cream cheese (8 oz)
  • Shredded cheddar cheese (1 cup)
  • All-purpose flour (1/2 cup)
  • Panko breadcrumbs (1/2 cup)

Directions

Step 1: Chicken Burgers

Preheat a pan or griddle to medium-high heat.

Mix together garlic powder, onion powder, and flour in a shallow dish.

Beat 1 egg in another shallow dish.

Place breadcrumbs in a third shallow dish.

Dip each chicken breast into the flour mixture, then the egg mixture, and then the breadcrumbs.

Place the coated chicken breasts onto the preheated pan or griddle and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.

Serve each chicken breast on a burger bun with your favorite toppings.

Step 2: Jalapeno Cheese Bites

Preheat a pan or griddle to medium-high heat.

Mix together grated cheese, cream cheese, and onion powder in a bowl.

Take a small round slice of jalapeno and place a small spoonful of the cheese mixture on top.

Roll the cheese mixture and jalapeno slice into a ball and repeat with remaining slices and cheese mixture.

Mix together flour and onion powder in a shallow dish.

Beat 1 egg in another shallow dish.

Place breadcrumbs in a third shallow dish.

Dip each cheese ball into the flour mixture, then the egg mixture, and then the breadcrumbs.

Place the coated cheese balls onto the preheated pan or griddle and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.

Serve hot and enjoy!
