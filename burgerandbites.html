Chicken burger & Cheese bites

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken (1lb)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Pepper (1 tsp)
  • Garlic powder (1 tsp)
  • Onion powder (1 tsp)
  • Breadcrumbs (1/2 cup)
  • Egg (1)
  • Buns (4)
  • Jalape単o peppers (6-8, depending on size)
  • Cream cheese (8 oz)
  • Shredded cheddar cheese (1 cup)
  • All-purpose flour (1/2 cup)
  • Panko breadcrumbs (1/2 cup)

Directions

Step 1:

In a large bowl, mix together the ground chicken, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, breadcrumbs, and egg. Form the mixture into 4 equal-sized patties.


Step 2:

Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side or until cooked through.


Step 3:

Toast the buns and assemble the burgers with your desired toppings.


Step 4

For the Jalape単o Cheese Bites: Cut the jalape単o peppers in half lengthwise and remove the seeds. Fill each pepper half with cream cheese and shredded cheddar cheese.


Step 5

In one shallow dish, place the flour and in another shallow dish, place the panko breadcrumbs. Dip each pepper half in the flour, then in the egg, and then in the panko breadcrumbs.


Step 6:

Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat and fry the jalape単o cheese bites for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels and serve warm.
