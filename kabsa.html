Kabsa

Ingredients

  • Carrot (2)
  • Tomatoes (400g/5)
  • Rice (400g)
  • Garlic (6 cloves)
  • Chicken legs + skin (1kg)
  • Tomato puree (70g/5 tbsp)
  • Onion (2)
  • Raisins (70g)
  • SPICES:
  • Black pepper (1 tsp)
  • Cardamon (1 tsp)
  • Tumeric (2 tsp)
  • Paprika (2 tsp)
  • Ginger (1 tbsp)
  • Cinnamon (2 tsp)
  • Coriander spice (1 tsp)

Directions

Step 1:

Chop the onions and 3 tomatoes into very small pieces, and grate the carrot and 4 garlic cloves. Then in a pan, heat oil, then add the onion and garlic and fry for 3-4 mins


Step 2:

Add the chicken into the mix, then add salt and all the spices onto the chicken, then add the tomatoes, grated carrot and 2 tbsp tomatoe puree. Mix and fry for 4-5 mins


Step 3:

Add 600ml of boiling water to the chicken mix and leave till the chicken is cooked (bone detaching from meat).


Step 5

Once the chicken is cooked take it out, add 1 ladle of the sauce with 1 tbsp of tomato puree together and glaze the chicken with it, then cook in the oven/air fryer 8-10 mins. Now add the rice to the sauce and cook on high heat for 1 min, then add salt and simmer till ready 10-15 mins


Step 4:

Meanwhile, blend 2 tomatoes and the pepper till it becomes a thick sauce. Then in a seperate pan, heat oil and add the rest of the garlic (grated) and fry for 1 min, then add 1 tbsp of vinegar, 1 tsp paprika, 2 tbsp of tomato puree and the blended tomatoes and pepper from before, and simmer for 5 mins.


Step 5:

Now pour the rice onto a plate, garnish with raisins and add the chicken ontop, and serve with the blended sauce as a dipping sauce.
