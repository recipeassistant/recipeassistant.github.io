Chicken Linguine

Ingredients

  • Lemon (1)
  • Shallot (2)
  • Linguine (500g)
  • Chicken (500g)
  • Peas (250g)
  • Creme fraishe (200g)
  • Parsley (handful)
  • Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Veg-stock (20g)

Directions

Step 1:

Boil water in a sauecpan with 1/2 tsp salt for the linguine. Zest and halve the lemon. Chop the parsley, Cut the shallot into small pieces and grate the garlic.


Step 2:

Whilst the linguine is cooking, heat oil in a pan and cook the shallot for 4-5 mins. Then add the garlic and fry for 1 min more.


Step 3:

Once the linguine is cooked drain and preserve the pasta water (amount above). then add the pasta water with the veg-stock to the shallot and garlic, bring to a boil and simmer for 3-4 mins.


Step 4:

Meanwhile, pre-cook the chicken, with 1 tblsp salt and pepper, in some oil in a seperate pan 4-7 mins till just cooked. then add the cooked chicken and peas into the sauce and cook for another 2-3 mins.


Step 5:

Then stir the creme fraiche and lemon zest into the sauce. Bring back to the boil, then remove from the heat. Season with pepper, then stir through the cooked pasta and half the parsley.
