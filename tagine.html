Lamb Tagine

Ingredients

  • Onions (4)
  • Peas (160g)
  • Lamb (600g)
  • Carrot (1)
  • Garlic (3 cloves)
  • Potato (1)
  • Tomato (3)
  • Pepper (1)
  • SPICES:
  • Paprika (1 Tbsp)
  • Ginger (1 Tbsp)
  • Tumeric (1 Tbsp)
  • Parsley (Handful)

Directions

Step 1:

Marinate the meat with salt and lemon juice and leave in the fridge ideally a few hours before cooking. meanwhile add the spices with some salt, black pepper and grated garlic in a bowl, add 2 tbsp of water and mix.


Step 2:

Take out the chicken and mix and marinate it in this spice mix, and leave for 10 mins. Meanwhile, Cut chop the tomatoes into small pieces, cut the onions into thin wing pieces (like a boomerang), and cut the carrot, pepper and potato into small pieces, ideally recatngle pieces.


Step 3:

Heat some oil in a clay tagine pan, and fry the chicken 2-4 mins flipping halfway until golden. Then begin layering the vegetables beginniing with the onions ontop of the meat, then layer the tomatoes next, then the peas next ontop, then layer the carrot, potato and pepper accross, building upwards like a pyramid, alternating between them.


Step 4:

Pour any of the remaining spice mix you made earlier ontop, sprinkle some salt and simmer on low heat with the lid closed for around 1 hour, whilst making sure to collect the sauce at the bottom and pour over everything every 10 mins. then turn the heat off and garnish with a handful of parsley, heat some arabic bread or garlic naan, whichever you prefer and enjoy.
