Sweet Chicken Noodles

Ingredients

  • Udon Noodles (500g)
  • Chicken (500g)
  • Garlic (4 cloves)
  • Sweet Soy Sauce (4 tbsp/50 ml)
  • Soy Sauce (4 tbsp/50 ml)
  • Spring Onion (200g)
  • Golden syrup (180g)
  • Flour (3 tbsp/20g)
  • Pepper (2 pieces)
  • Vinegar (6 tbsp/75ml)

Directions

Step 1:

Add your udon noodles to a large pot with some oil, then add just enough water to barely cover the noodles and simmer till nearly cooked 3-5 mins.


Step 2:

Then add 2 Tblsp of bbq seasoning or these powders:(garlic, cumin, onion, chilli, paprika, black pepper, ginger, thyme) mix and keep simmering till its done and the waters gone 3-4 mins.


Step 3:

Meanwhile chop your spring onions and pepper into small pieces and grate the garlic. Now to prepare the chicken sauce. Into a bowl add the Soy sauce, the sweet soy sauce, vinegar and golden syrup. Mix well and set aside.


Step 4:

Now in a large bowl, add the chicken, coat with the flour, season with salt and pepper and mix. Then heat abit of oil in a pan and fry the chicken till any redness is gone and becomes golden.


Step 5:

Then add a spoon of butter and the garlic, mix and fry for 1 min. then add the golden-soy sauce you set aside earlier, stir and cook for another 4-5 mins till the chicken is cooked and sauce has thickened.


Step 6:

Meanwhile, in a seperate pan, heat abit of oil, add the vegetabes and fry till cooked 3-4 mins. Then add the cooked veg to the cooked noodles. Finally pour the noodles into plates or bowls, add the sweet saucy chicken and Serve!
