Sweet Chicken Noodles

Ingredients

  • Udon Noodles (400g)
  • Chicken (500g)
  • Garlic (4 cloves)
  • Sweet Soy Sauce (6 tbsp/85 ml)
  • Soy Sauce (6 tbsp/85 ml)
  • Spring Onion (200g)
  • Honey (4 tbsp/70g)
  • Flour (3 tbsp/20g)
  • Pepper (2 pieces)
  • Vinegar (6 tbsp/75ml)

Directions

Step 1:

Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil. Add your udon noodles with enough water to cover them and simmer till nearly cooked 7-8 mins. Meanwhile chop your spring onions and pepper into small pieces and grate the garlic.


Step 2:

Now to prepare the noodle sauce and chicken sauce. First, into a bowl add the Soy sauce, the sweet soy sauce, ginger, garlic, vinegar and honey. Mix well and set aside.


Step 3:

Now, Once the noodles are nearly cooked, drain most of the water and leave around half a cups worth. Then add half of the honey-soy sauce you set aside earlier, mix well and cook for another 2-3 mins till absorbed.


Step 4:

Now in a large bowl, add the chicken, coat with the flour, season with salt and pepper and mix. Then heat abit of oil in a pan and fry the chicken till any redness is gone and becomes a light brown colour. Then add the other half of the honey-soy sauce, stir and cook for another 4-5 mins till the chicken is cooked and has absorbed some of the sauce.


Step 5:

Meanwhile, in a seperate pan, heat abit of oil, add the vegetabes and fry till cooked 3-4 mins. Then add the cooked veg to the cooked noodles. Finally pour the noodles into plates or bowls, add the chicken and Serve!
