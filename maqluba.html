Maqluba

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (500g)
  • Rice (400g)
  • Onion (1)
  • Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Cumin (1 tsp)
  • Cinnamon (1 tsp)
  • Diced tomatoes (400g)

Directions

Step 1:

In a pot, heat the oil over medium heat and saut√© the onions and garlic until softened.

Step 2:

Add the meat and cook until browned on all sides.

Step 3:

Stir in the cumin, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Add the rice and stir to coat the rice with the spices. Add the tomatoes, peas, and broth. Bring to a boil and reduce the heat to low.

Step 4:

Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through and the liquid has been absorbed.

Step 5:

Turn off the heat and let the pot sit, covered, for 5 minutes.

Step 6:

To serve, place a large platter on top of the pot and carefully invert the pot so that the maqluba falls onto the platter.
