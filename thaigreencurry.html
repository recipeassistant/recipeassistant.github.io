Thai Green Curry

Ingredients

  • Green-Beans (100g)
  • Chicken (500g)
  • Rice (400g)
  • Coconut-Milk (400ml)
  • Thai-Green-Paste (40g)
  • Pepper (2 pieces)
  • Soy-Sauce (30ml)
  • Coriander (handful)

Directions

Step 1:

Trim the green beans and chop into thirds.- Halve the red pepper and discard the core and seeds. Slice into thin strips. - Roughly chop the coriander (stalks and all).


Step 2:

Heat abit of oil in a deep saucepan and add the rice with 3 cups of boiling water, 1 tblsp of salt and simmer for approx 15 mins till cooked.


Step 3:

Meanwhile, Heat a splash of oil in a large frying pan on medium-high heat. Add the chicken and stir-fry until starting to brown, 3-4 mins. Add the pepper and green beans and stir-fry for another minute.


Step 4:

Stir in the Thai green curry paste and cook for 30 seconds. Pour in the coconut milk, stir to dissolve the paste Lower the heat to medium, cover with a lid (or foil) and simmer until the chicken is cooked through, 8-10 mins and the veg is tender.


Step 5:

-Once the curry is cooked, you can add abit of yoghurt or flour to thicken it. Then remove from the heat and stir through the soy sauce. Season to taste with salt and pepper, add lime if you like.


Step 6:

Share the rice between your bowls topped with the curry and a sprinkling of coriander and Enjoy!
